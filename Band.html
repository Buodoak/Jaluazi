Welcome to our page!!!
Jaluazi is comprised of three brothers; Jani, Jalazi and Jua from a small town called Gendia.
We were introduced to music by our late mother who was a member of a local band in Gendia.
She was the lead singer of the group but also played instruments.
She specialized in traditional instrument of nyatiti and orutu.
We accompanied her to her practise and performances.
These got us appreciating different parts of music while also inspiring us into music.
We specialize in traditional music both acoustic and electrical.
